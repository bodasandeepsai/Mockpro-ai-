Project Overview

MockPro-AI is an intelligent interview preparation platform that:

  • Simulates realistic job interviews using Gemini AI
  • Generates tailored interview questions based on job positions
  • Provides real-time feedback and performance analysis
  • Supports both technical and non-technical roles
  • Helps candidates improve their interviewing skills through practice

Problem Statement

Job interviews are stressful and unpredictable

Candidates often feel unprepared and anxious, leading to poor performance even when they have the right skills.

Lack of personalized preparation options

Generic interview prep doesn't account for specific job roles, industries, or experience levels.

Technical interviews require specialized practice

Coding questions and technical assessments need domain-specific preparation that's hard to find.

High cost of interview coaching

Professional interview coaches charge $100+ per hour, making quality preparation inaccessible to many.

Difficulty getting objective feedback

Friends and family may not provide honest, constructive criticism on interview performance.

Solution: MockPro-AI

An accessible, AI-powered interview simulator that:

Personalized Question Generation

Helps users monitor performance improvements over time.

Implementation Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: API Reliability

Gemini API would occasionally timeout or hit rate limits during question generation.

Solution:

Implemented retry mechanisms with exponential backoff, timeout controls, and pre-generated fallback content for seamless user experience.

Challenge: Speech Recognition Accuracy

Speech recognition quality varied based on accents, background noise, and network conditions.

Solution:

Added real-time text display during speech for user confidence and a manual input option as a fallback.

Challenge: Technical vs Non-Technical Question Generation

Different job roles required entirely different question types and evaluation criteria.

Solution:

Developed an intelligent role detection algorithm using keyword analysis in job titles and descriptions.
